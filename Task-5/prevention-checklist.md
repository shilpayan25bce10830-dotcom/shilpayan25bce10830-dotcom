\\# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

\\## For College Students in India

\\## General Cybersecurity Tips

1\\. Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers,

and symbols. Never reuse passwords across platforms.

2\\. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important

accounts like email, banking, and social media.

3\\. Never click on suspicious links received via SMS,

WhatsApp, or email — even if they look official.

4\\. Always verify the identity of anyone who contacts you

claiming to be from a bank or government agency.

5\\. Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against

security vulnerabilities.

6\\. Do not share personal information like Aadhaar number,

PAN, or date of birth on unknown websites.

\\## UPI and Financial Safety Tips

7\\. NEVER share your UPI PIN with anyone — not even bank

officials. No legitimate person will ever ask for it.

8\\. Remember: entering your UPI PIN always means SENDING

money, never receiving it. Be alert for collect requests.

\\## Reporting Channels

\\- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

\\- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7, all India)

\\- Report immediately if you are a victim — faster reporting

increases chances of recovering lost money.
