# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO'S
1. Keep your profile professional and up to date
2. Use strong and unique passwords for each platform
3. Think before you post — once online, always online
4. Follow educational and career-related pages
5. Report cyberbullying or suspicious activity immediately

## DON'TS
1. Don't share personal information like address or phone number
2. Don't accept friend requests from unknown people
3. Don't post negative comments about classmates or teachers
4. Don't click on suspicious links or pop-up ads
5. Don't use the same password across multiple platforms
